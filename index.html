Flowers

India is one of the nations that give much importance to preserve environment and pray each tree and plant as Gods and Goddesses. In Indian mythology also there is a lot of connection between flowers and its different kinds of stories with deities. Flowers occupy a unique place in every creature's mind and life. The strong aroma of flowers has aesthetic values, medicinal values and it also has the healing power to cure mental stress, depression, anxiety, and weariness.
